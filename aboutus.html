From Vision to Reality

FontDale was first conceptualized in 2023 by visionary developer Franz Diego, who recognized the need for a premium lifestyle destination that seamlessly blends contemporary architecture with world-class retail experiences.

After two years of planning and construction, FontDale officially opened its doors in 2025, quickly establishing itself as the premium shopping destination in the region.

Located at the prestigious Arcadia Circle, FontDale represents more than just a shopping mall, it is a carefully crafted environment where luxury, comfort, and convenience converge to create an unforgettable experience.

Meet Our Founder

Franz Diego is a renowned real estate developer and entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience in creating landmark properties that transform communities.

His passion for excellence and attention to detail is evident in every aspect of FontDale, from the architectural design to the carefully curated tenant mix. Franz believes that a shopping destination should offer more than transactions; it should provide experiences that enrich people's lives.

"FontDale is not just about shopping. It's about creating a space where people can discover, connect, and enjoy the finest offerings in an environment designed for their comfort and delight." - Franz Diego

What Makes FontDale Unique

Discover the distinctive features that set us apart from other shopping destinations

Architectural Excellence

FontDale features contemporary design with premium finishes throughout. The signature Lumen Skywalk connects the North Gallery and South Pavilion, offering a stunning glass-enclosed pathway with natural light and artistic installations.

Curated Tenant Mix

We carefully select each tenant to ensure a diverse and premium shopping experience. From international luxury brands to unique local boutiques, every store is chosen to complement our vision of excellence and variety.

Premium Services

Enjoy personalized concierge services, valet parking, complimentary Wi-Fi, family lounges, and exclusive VIP shopping experiences. We ensure every visit to FontDale is comfortable and memorable.
